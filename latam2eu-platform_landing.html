The Problem

Exporting from LATAM to the EU is broken.

Brazilian agricultural producers with real product and real volume are failing at the European border — not because of the goods, but because of the paperwork, the opacity, and the middlemen who don't scale.

📋
Documentation chaos
Dozens of regulatory documents, each with different deadlines, formats, and authorities — tracked via email and spreadsheets.
🚢
Opaque freight costs
Freight forwarders (transitários) are the only source of pricing — no visibility, no comparison, no data.
🔌
No EU connections
LATAM producers have no direct access to European distributors, resellers, or end-clients — the entire supply chain is invisible to them.
⚠️
Port detentions & rejections
A single missing document causes cargo to be held at the Portuguese port — costing thousands in demurrage fees.
The Mission System

One export. One Mission. Total clarity.

Every export event becomes a structured Mission — a single operational hub covering compliance, documentation, logistics, and partner connections.

01
Create Mission
Define product, volume, origin port, EU destination, and target timeline.
02
Score & Verify
Upload documents. The platform scores your preparedness against EU/Mercosul rules.
03
Match Partners
Connect with freighters, importers, distributors, and resellers in the EU network.
04
Ship & Track
Execute the shipment with all parties aligned and documentation verified.

Platform Features

Built around the real workflow.

📊
Preparedness Score Engine
Every document you upload is validated against EU regulatory requirements and Mercosul agreements. A real-time score tells you exactly what's missing before you ship.
🗂️
Compliance Document Hub
We centralize all Mercosul–EU legal requirements — phytosanitary rules, customs declarations, EUR.1 certificates — and match them against your exports automatically.
💹
Freight Tariff Intelligence
Eliminate the transitário dependency. Access reference tariff data by route, cargo type, and season to compare shipping costs transparently.
🤝
Partner Matchmaking
EU freighters, importers, distributors, and end resellers can discover your missions and initiate negotiations — directly, without intermediaries.
📡
Admin & Partner Portals
Separate portals for exporters, EU partners, and platform operators. Each role sees exactly what they need — nothing more, nothing less.
